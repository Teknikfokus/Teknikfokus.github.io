Teknikfokus

Teknikfokus is a fair for the Computer and Electrical Engineering guilds at Lund University of Technology. The goal of the fair is to give the participating companies an opportunity to show concrete products and/or services that they work with. Just as the name suggests, the fair focuses on technology. This will give the students an insight into what one as a completed civil engineer can work with and give the companies a chance to show off their unique technology solutions.
