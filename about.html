About us
Teknikfokus is a career fair arranged by students in the E and D-guilds at Lunds Tekniska Högskola. The fair aims to connect students who study Computer Science, Information and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Biomedical Engineering with companies in their fields of interest. In addition to the fair we provide opportunities for lunch lectures, evening events and student sessions.

Our goal is to provide a platform where both students and companies can gain insights in their specialised fields.

The project group consists of students for all four programs with one project manager from each guild. The project group has been working together since july. Below you can find all the members of the project group, as well as their contact information and field of responsibility.

Take a look at our FAQ to find out more about Teknikfokus.
