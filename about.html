Teknikfokus

Teknikfokus is a career fair dedicated towards students in the E and D-guilds at LTH, which includes students who study Computer Science, Information and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Biomedical Engineering.

Our goal is to create a platform that, in the best possible way, connects students at LTH with companies and interesting people. We want to inspire students and help them learn so they become KICKASS - thereby create opportunities for both companies and students.

Since we are students from CS, EE, BME, we focus on Technology, hence the name "Teknikfokus". We believe that Teknikfokus should be a fair where both students and companies can gain insights in their specialised fields.

Take a look at FAQ to find out more about Teknikfokus.
